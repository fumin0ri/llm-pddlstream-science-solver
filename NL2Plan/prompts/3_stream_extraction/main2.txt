## Domain and Problem

Please solve the following problem.

A small car of mass 800 kg starts from rest and accelerates uniformly to a speed of 20 m/s in 10 seconds on a flat road. During this time, the engine provides a constant power output, and the resistive force acting on the car is 400 N. What is the power output of the engine? (Ignore air resistance other than the 400 N resistive force.)

In Requires, you may write only predicates that are already defined in ## Init Facts or those produced in a previous stream.

In Produces, you may not use predicate names from ## Init Facts or from the previous stream’s Produces; you must rename them to distinguish them.

```
dist_from_at2

Computes displacement under uniform acceleration.  
Formula: s = ½ a t²  
Requires accel and time  
Produces distance 
```

```
dke_from_mv

Computes change in kinetic energy.  
Formula: ΔK = ½ m v²  
Requires mass and velocity  
Produces dK
```

```
wres_from_fs

Computes work against resistive force.  
Formula: W_resist = F_resist × s  
Requires resistive_Force and distance.  
Produces W_resist 
```

```
work_total

Combines total work done by the engine.  
Formula: W_total = ΔK + W_resist  
Requires dK and W_resist.  
Produces W_total
```

```
power_from_wt

Computes constant power output.  
Formula: P = W_total / t  
Requires W_total and time.  
Produces  Answer and (done) ;This is the final stream.
```
