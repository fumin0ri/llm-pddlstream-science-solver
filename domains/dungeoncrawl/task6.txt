In this dungeon, five locations are connected bidirectionally in a line: vault-hoard-gate-cellar-sewer. There is monk and an inquisitor by the gate and a warpriest in the sewer. Additionally, there is a dragon at the hoard, a hatchling in the vault and an ooze in the sewer. The monk is carrying a staff any adventurer can use, but there is also a scythe (usable by the inquisitor) and a battle axe (usable by the inquisitor or warpriest) in the hoard. Additionally, the vault and cellar are trapped though the monk can cross these.
