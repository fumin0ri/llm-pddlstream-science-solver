In this problem, there is an exit bidirectionally connected to a planetarium, a prison and a forest. The planetarium is also bidirectionally connected to the trapped study and the prison. A halfling (who can cross traps) is in the planetarium. A wizard who can use staffs (one is in the study) and wands (one is in the planetarium) is in the prison. Additionally, there is a hostile guard in the prison and one by the exit. The goal is for the halfling to reach the forest.
