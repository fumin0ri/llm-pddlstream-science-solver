In the current dungeon, all locations are fully connected. The only adventurer is a magus located in the prison. They can use any weapon and is carrying a dagger. Additionally, there is both a scimitar and a crossbow in the armory as well as an ornamental blade in the shrine. There is a minotaur in the prison, a golem in the armory and a wyrm in the shrine. The goal is for the magus to get the ornate blade and escape to the tunnels.
