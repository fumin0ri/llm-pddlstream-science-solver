In this card game, you can draw cards from a deck and play cards from your hand. You can only draw if you have at most one card in hand. Only cards with effects can be played and only if the effect can be performed. When played, a card can cause any of three effects, depending on the card: Discard, Mill, or Cycle. "Discard" makes you discard the other card in your hand. "Mill" makes you discard the top card of the deck. "Cycle" places the other card in your hand at the bottom of the deck. If a card has multiple effects, you must choose one when playing it. The goal is to play or discard all cards in the deck.
